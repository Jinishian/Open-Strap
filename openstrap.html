RMSSD — parasympathetic (vagal) activity; higher = better recovery.   SD1 — beat-to-beat variability (perpendicular to Poincaré identity line).   SD2 — long-term variability (along identity line).   LF/HF — sympathovagal balance; <1.5 = parasympathetic, >2.5 = sympathetic dominance. Frequency domain requires ~2 min of continuous R-R data. All metrics are estimated approximations, not clinical measurements.
